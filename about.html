About Us

Our Story

Celebrations and events like weddings and anniversaries, Birthday parties, and corporate gatherings are common occurrences in life and maybe incredibly demanding to prepare, especially if guests are coming from all over the nation. Srinivasan event management has an expert team of event planners who will help you every step of the way if you have no idea where to start or the best course of action to take.

Led by renowned planners, Srinivasan and Lakshmi Priya with more than a decade of experience leading a professional team who work with you from start to finish to ensure that all of your needs and desires are met so that you can host one of the most memorable celebrations that your guests will remember for a long time.

we provide the greatest service possible to our clients, working tirelessly and completely devotedly to create a one-of-a-kind for your entire event, making sure we have all the information you want to include in the program. We offer all kinds of event services at affordable prices. Srinivasan event management uses trend fashion to make your events unique and extraordinary.
